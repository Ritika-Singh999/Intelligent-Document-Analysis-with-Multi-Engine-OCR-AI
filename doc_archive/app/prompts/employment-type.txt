Determine employment type based on document evidence:

Types:
1. Employee
2. Self-employed
3. Public Servant
4. Student
5. Retired
6. Unemployed

Required evidence per type:
- Employee: Payslips, employment contract, social security records
- Self-employed: Tax declarations, VAT returns, professional licenses
- Public Servant: Government appointment, public sector payslips
- Student: Enrollment certificates, student ID, scholarship documents
- Retired: Pension statements, retirement benefits
- Unemployed: Unemployment benefits, job seeker registration

Classification rules:
- Require minimum 2 supporting documents per type
- Consider document dates and validity
- Note any conflicts or inconsistencies
- Default to "Not Found" if insufficient evidence
