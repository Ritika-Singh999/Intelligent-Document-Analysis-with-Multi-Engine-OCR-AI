Extract and analyze income information from financial documents:

Analysis points:
1. Primary Income Sources
   - Salary/wages
   - Business income
   - Professional fees
   - Commission/bonuses
   - Investment returns

2. Income Classification
   - Employment income
   - Self-employment revenue
   - Passive income
   - Government benefits
   - Pension/retirement

3. Income Patterns
   - Regular monthly income
   - Quarterly payments
   - Annual bonuses
   - Seasonal variations
   - Project-based income

4. Key Metrics
   - Gross income
   - Net income
   - Tax withholdings
   - Social security contributions
   - Income stability

5. Documentation Types
   - Payslips
   - Bank statements
   - Tax returns
   - Invoices
   - Contract payments

Output format:
- Total income per category
- Monthly average income
- Income source breakdown
- Stability assessment
- Growth/decline trends
- Income verification status
