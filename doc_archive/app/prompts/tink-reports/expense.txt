Extract and analyze expense information from financial documents:

Analysis points:
1. Regular Expenses
   - Housing (rent/mortgage)
   - Utilities
   - Insurance payments
   - Transportation costs
   - Food and groceries
   - Healthcare expenses

2. Business Expenses (if self-employed)
   - Office supplies/equipment
   - Professional services
   - Business travel
   - Marketing/advertising
   - Software/subscriptions

3. Expense Patterns
   - Monthly recurring charges
   - Quarterly payments
   - Annual expenses
   - Irregular large purchases

4. Categories to Track
   - Essential vs. non-essential
   - Business vs. personal
   - Tax-deductible items
   - Debt payments

Output format:
- Total expenses per category
- Monthly average spend
- Expense trends
- Notable patterns
- Irregular transactions
- Red flags or anomalies
